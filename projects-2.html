TaskTrove

Project overview

TaskTrove is a mobile application designed to address the organizational challenges faced by students juggling academic responsibilities with work commitments. By providing a comprehensive suite of features, TaskTrove aims to enhance productivity, time management, and motivation among its users. The app incentivizes task completion through a unique reward system, where users earn virtual coins redeemable for online purchases, thereby fostering a sense of achievement and progress.

User Persona

My friend Joseph, is a college student pursuing a degree in Information Technology while also working part-time as a freelance web developer. Joseph struggles to balance her academic assignments with her work commitments, often feeling overwhelmed by the sheer volume of tasks she needs to complete. She seeks a solution that not only helps her stay organized but also provides motivation to tackle her tasks efficiently.

Features

  • Landing Page: Upon logging in, users are greeted with a comprehensive overview of their upcoming tasks. The landing page displays a list of pending assignments, deadlines, and prioritized tasks, allowing users like Joseph to plan their schedule effectively.
  • Task Details: Users can delve into the specifics of each task, accessing detailed descriptions, deadlines, and associated materials. Additionally, they have the flexibility to add new tasks, update existing ones, or remove tasks as needed, providing a dynamic platform for managing academic responsibilities.
  • Adjustable Importance and Difficulty Levels: To cater to varying preferences and personal goals, TaskTrove allows users to adjust the importance and difficulty levels of their tasks. This feature empowers students to challenge themselves, strike a balance between demanding and manageable tasks, and optimize their productivity accordingly.
  • Timer and Time Tracking: Efficient time management is crucial for academic success, which is why TaskTrove includes a built-in timer feature. Users can set timers for individual tasks, track the time spent on each activity, and analyze their productivity patterns over time, facilitating better time allocation and workflow optimization.
  • Achievements: TaskTrove encourages productivity by utilizing a set of goals and accomplishments. If users meet targets, make steady progress, and do exceptionally well, they can receive virtual badges, coin, and recognition for reaching specific goals. These accomplishments motivate them to keep getting better and act as material rewards for their efforts.
  • Shop: TaskTrove stands out for its integrated virtual shop, where users can exchange the cash they receive for finishing tasks. From lifestyle products and entertainment options to productivity tools and educational resources, the shop offers a wide variety of goods, services, and experiences. Users like Joseph are encouraged by this reward system to stick with the program, aim for greatness, and see real results from their academic pursuits.

Expected Benefits

  • Increased Academic Performance: TaskTrove streamlines task management, enhances time allocation, and motivates users to stay focused on their studies. This results in better grades, increased subject mastery, and a sense of accomplishment.

Conclusion

TaskTrove represents a holistic solution for students seeking to enhance their organizational skills, time management abilities, and motivation levels. By combining practical task management features with a rewarding incentive system, the app empowers users to optimize their academic performance, achieve their goals, and enjoy a fulfilling learning experience. As Joseph and countless other students embrace TaskTrove as their academic companion, they embark on a journey of productivity, growth, and success.
